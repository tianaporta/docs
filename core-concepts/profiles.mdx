## What are Profiles?

Profiles store the billing, shipping, and payment info Halo uses to complete checkouts. Every task needs a profile attached — without one, there's nothing to fill into the checkout form.

Profiles in Halo are grouped, so you can keep test profiles separate from real ones, or organize by region, friend, family member, etc.

## Creating a Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Profiles tab">
    Head to the **Profiles** tab in the top nav.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a group">
    Click the **+** next to **Groups** and name your group (e.g. `Personal`, `Tiana`, `EU Profiles`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click + New Profile">
    Hit the **+ New Profile** button at the bottom of the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in profile details">
    Enter the shipping address, billing address (toggle **Same as Shipping Address** if they match), and contact info.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add payment info">
    Enter the card number, expiration, and CVV. Halo stores this locally — your payment data never leaves your machine.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Create Profile">
    Done. The profile is ready to attach to tasks.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Profile Fields

**Profile Details**

- **Profile Name** — internal name for the profile (e.g. `Tiana NY`, `John Backup`)
- **First Name / Last Name** — used for billing and shipping
- **Email** — checkout email (used for order confirmations and IMAP if applicable)
- **Street Address** — main shipping address
- **Address 2** — apartment, suite, or unit number
- **Country / State / City / ZIP** — shipping location
- **Phone** — checkout phone number

**Payment Info**

- **Card Number** — 16-digit card number
- **Exp Month / Exp Year** — card expiration
- **CVV** — 3 or 4 digit security code

<Note>
**Same as Shipping Address** is on by default. Toggle it off if your billing address differs from your shipping address.
</Note>

## Managing Profiles

- **Import / Export** — bulk move profiles in and out using the buttons in the top right
- **Search** — find specific profiles inside a group
- **Change Group** — move selected profiles to a different group
- **Delete** — remove selected profiles

## Tips

- **Use real info.** Fake names and addresses get orders flagged and canceled.
- **One card per profile** is the cleanest setup — splitting a card across multiple profiles can trigger fraud checks.
- **Group by purpose.** Keep personal profiles separate from family/friend profiles for easier management.
- **Match profile country to site region** for cleanest checkouts (e.g. US profiles on US sites).
- **Phone numbers matter** on some sites — use real numbers to avoid checkout failures.

## Security

Halo stores all profile and payment data **locally on your machine**. Nothing is uploaded to a server, shared with the team, or accessible to anyone but you.
