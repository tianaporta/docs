Halo is distributed through Whop. This page covers buying access, finding your license, and managing renewals.

## Purchasing Halo

Halo is sold on Whop with a one-time fee plus a quarterly subscription. Once payment goes through, you'll get instant access to the Halo dashboard and an invite to the Halo Discord.

<Note>
Halo opens in waves. If renewals are closed, joining the waitlist is the way in.
</Note>

## Getting Your License Key

After purchase, head to [whop.com/joined/halo-bot/get-your-key](https://whop.com/joined/halo-bot/get-your-key-CJivB2gmRUCHEk/app/) and copy your key. You'll paste this into Halo the first time you launch the app.

## Managing Your Subscription

- **Renewals** are handled automatically through Whop
- **Cancellation** can be done from your Whop dashboard at any time
- **Payment method updates** are also done through Whop, not Halo

<Warning>
If your subscription lapses, Halo will stop working until you renew. Keep your payment method current.
</Warning>

## Transferring Your License

Licenses are tied to your Whop account and aren't transferable. If you sell, lose, or share access, your license can be revoked.

## Need Help With Whop?

For Whop-specific issues, use Whop's support directly from your dashboard. For Halo-specific issues, drop into the Halo Discord `#support` channel.
