This guide walks you through everything you need to run your first task.

## What You'll Need

<Steps>
  <Step title="Halo License Key">
    Grab your key here: [whop.com/joined/halo-bot/get-your-key](https://whop.com/joined/halo-bot/get-your-key-CJivB2gmRUCHEk/app/)
  </Step>
  <Step title="A Profile">
    Your billing and shipping info. You'll create this inside Halo.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Proxies">
    Most sites need them. ISP or residential is the default recommendation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="An IMAP-enabled email">
    Required for sites with email verification. See the [IMAP guide](/core-concepts/imap).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 1: Download Halo

- **Windows** — [HaloBot-win-latest.exe](https://halo-dl.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/HaloBot-win-latest.exe)
- **Mac** — [HaloBot-mac-latest.dmg](https://halo-dl.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/HaloBot-mac-latest.dmg)

Run the installer and follow the prompts.

## Step 2: Activate Halo

Open Halo. Paste your license key when prompted. Once authenticated, the dashboard loads.

## Step 3: Create Your First Profile

Head to the **Profiles** tab in the top nav.

Click the **+** next to **Groups** to create a profile group, then hit **New Profile**.

Fill in your profile details — name, shipping address, billing address, and payment info — then click **Create Profile**.

## Step 4: Add Proxies

1. Open the **Proxies** tab
2. Click **Create Group** and name it (e.g. `Residential 1`)
3. Paste your proxies in the format `ip:port:user:pass`
4. Save

<Note>
Some modules work without proxies on small task counts. For any serious drop, use proxies.
</Note>

## Step 5: Create Your First Task

Head to the **Tasks** tab.

Click the **+** next to **Groups** to create a task group. Name it and pick your task type (Shopify, Amazon, PKC, etc.).

Hit **Confirm**, then click **+ New Task** at the bottom of the page. Fill in:

- **Site** — the store you're running
- **Mode** — Normal, Safe, Fast, Super Safe, or App
- **Keywords/URL** — product ID or keyword
- **Size** — leave blank or set to Random if you don't need a specific size
- **Profiles** — the profile(s) to use
- **Accounts** — if the site needs login

Hit **Create Task**.

## Step 6: Start the Task

Hit **Start** on the task. Halo will begin running.

That's it. You're live.
