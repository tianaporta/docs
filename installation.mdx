## Download

Pick the installer for your operating system:

- **Windows** — [HaloBot-win-latest.exe](https://halo-dl.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/HaloBot-win-latest.exe)
- **Mac** — [HaloBot-mac-latest.dmg](https://halo-dl.s3.us-east-1.amazonaws.com/HaloBot-mac-latest.dmg)

## Install

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Windows">
    1. Double-click `HaloBot-win-latest.exe`
    2. If Windows shows a SmartScreen warning, click **More info** → **Run anyway**
    3. Follow the installer prompts
    4. Launch Halo from your Start menu or desktop shortcut
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Mac">
    1. Double-click `HaloBot-mac-latest.dmg`
    2. Drag the Halo app into your Applications folder
    3. Open Halo from Applications
    4. If macOS blocks the app, go to **System Settings → Privacy & Security** and click **Open Anyway** for Halo
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## First Launch

When you open Halo for the first time, you'll be prompted for your license key.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Grab your license key">
    Head to [whop.com/joined/halo-bot/get-your-key](https://whop.com/joined/halo-bot/get-your-key-CJivB2gmRUCHEk/app/) and copy your key.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste and activate">
    Paste your key into Halo's activation screen. Once verified, the dashboard loads.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Windows SmartScreen blocking the installer">
    Click **More info** then **Run anyway**. Halo is unsigned for now — this is normal.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Mac says Halo can't be opened">
    Go to **System Settings → Privacy & Security**, scroll down, and click **Open Anyway** next to the Halo block notice.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="License key not activating">
    Make sure you copied the full key with no extra spaces. If it still fails, head to the Halo Discord `#support` channel.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
