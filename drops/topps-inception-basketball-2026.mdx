## About

Topps is releasing pre-orders for 2025-26 Topps Inception Basketball.

- **$289.99** per Hobby Box
- **$2,319.99** per Sealed Case (8 boxes)
- 1 autograph or autograph-relic per box
- Ships/releases in ~1 month

This is one of the better Topps Basketball releases in a while. $290 for 1 auto isn't terrible given how premium these cards are.

<Note>
**Why this matters:** First licensed Inception drop. Flagg RCs and LeBron autos in the set. Baseball Inception flopped, but basketball has more upside here.
</Note>

<Warning>
**FDI context:** The FDI version sold out last week at $1,050 (with 1 extra guaranteed auto). Big gap between FDI floor and tomorrow's retail — good sign for margins.
</Warning>
