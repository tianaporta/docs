## Drop Info

- **Retail** — $24.95

## About

Starbucks is releasing a new FIFA 2026 World Cup Bearista — a soccer-themed bear with a beanie/hat and scarf. Online exclusive at [shop.starbucks.com](https://www.shop.starbucks.com/) for U.S. customers.

<Warning>
This release is **exclusive to Starbucks Rewards Reserve tier members**. You need to have earned at least 2,500 Stars in the last 12 months (~$2,500 in consistent purchases) to be eligible. Check your tier before the drop.
</Warning>

## Site Type

1. Create a new task group
2. Select **Shopify**

## Task Setup

- **Site** — `Custom Shopify` → paste `https://www.shop.starbucks.com/`
- **Mode** — Normal
- **Keywords** — `starbucks bearista`
- **Profiles** — your usual profiles
- **Accounts** — Starbucks Rewards Reserve account required

## Notes

- Start tasks 5–10 minutes before drop so your timer loads
- Make sure your Starbucks Reserve account is logged in and active before drop time
- List on all platforms after copping and allow international shipping for more flexibility
- Previous Bearista release sold out instantly — expect heavy traffic

## Recommended Setup

- **Proxies** — Residential recommended given site traffic
- **Tasks Count** — match to the number of Reserve accounts you have
- Pair with the [Shopify module guide](/modules/shopify) for full module details
