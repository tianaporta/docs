## Overview

EQL runs raffle-style releases for hyped sneaker and apparel drops. Halo automates raffle entries across multiple accounts so you get more shots at winning.

## Account Setup

EQL tasks **require** an account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account group">
    Head to the **Accounts** tab in Halo. Click the **+** to create a new group. Name it (e.g. `EQL 1`) and pick **EQL** as the account type. Hit **Confirm**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste your accounts">
    Click **Add Accounts** and paste in `email:password` format, one per line.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Task Setup

- **Site** — EQL
- **Item URL** — the full URL of the EQL raffle page. Toggle **Bulk tasks** to paste multiple URLs at once.
- **Size** — specific size, or `Random` for any
- **Profiles** — pick the profile(s) to use
- **Accounts** *(Required)* — pick the EQL account(s) to enter with
- **Task Count** — `match accounts` (one entry per account) or `match profiles`

## Recommended Setup

- One task per account — more accounts = more raffle entries = better odds
- Use unique profiles per account when possible to avoid duplicate-entry flags
- Proxies: ISP or Residential recommended

## Notes

- EQL is a raffle, not a first-come-first-served checkout — running 50 tasks on one account won't help you, but 50 accounts with 1 task each will
- Account quality matters; aged accounts perform better than fresh ones
- Halo will only enter the raffle; winning still depends on the EQL draw
