## Overview

Halo supports Funko across every regional storefront. Use Normal mode for standard releases and Raffle Entry for invite-only raffle drops.

## Supported Sites

- **FunkoBE** — Belgium
- **FunkoCA** — Canada
- **FunkoDE** — Germany
- **FunkoES** — Spain
- **FunkoFR** — France
- **FunkoGB** — United Kingdom
- **FunkoHU** — Hungary
- **FunkoIT** — Italy
- **And more across EU and APAC** — search the Site picker for the one you need

<Note>
Each region is its own site. Match the profile country to the storefront for cleanest checkouts.
</Note>

## Account Setup

Funko tasks **require** an account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account group">
    Head to the **Accounts** tab in Halo. Click the **+** to create a new group. Name it (e.g. `Funko 1`) and pick **Funko** as the account type. Hit **Confirm**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste your accounts">
    Click **Add Accounts** and paste in `email:password` format, one per line.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Task Setup

- **Site** — pick the Funko regional storefront (e.g. `FunkoGB`, `FunkoCA`)
- **Mode** — Normal or Raffle Entry
- **PID** — product ID. Toggle **Bulk tasks** to paste multiple at once.
- **Size** — leave blank or use `Random` if there's no size variant
- **Profiles** — pick the profile(s) to use
- **Accounts** *(Required)* — pick the logged-in Funko account(s)
- **Task Count** — `match accounts` (one task per account) or `match profiles`

## Modes

### Normal (Standard)
Standard checkout flow. Adds to cart and checks out using the saved payment on the account.

### Raffle Entry (Entry)
Used for invite-only or raffle-style Funko drops. Enters the raffle using the account — does not checkout.

## Recommended Setup

- One task per account on raffle drops (more accounts = more entries = better odds)
- Multiple Normal tasks per account on standard restocks
- **PID over keyword:** Funko is PID-driven
- **Proxies:** ISP or Residential recommended
- **Delays:** default values are fine
- Match profile country to the Funko regional site

## Notes

- Funko enforces one entry per account on raffles — duplicate entries get flagged
- Aged accounts perform better than fresh ones on raffles
- Halo handles captchas automatically
- Account quality and proxy quality matter more than task count on raffles
